Attracting good quality talents is a serious challenge faced by many employers today. As good quality talents are usually "passive" candidates who are doing well in their companies and hardly (or never) have time to job-hunt, it becomes even more challenging for employers to find them.

Yellow Elevator understands that there is a need for a smart, efficient, yet cost-effective solution in finding this group of so-called "passive" candidates, and has found a way to identify them by implementing the job referral model through the Internet. This is how YellowElevator.com came about.

Yellow Elevator helps Employers:

  • Reach out to "passive" candidates
  • Reduce hiring costs with our competitive rates
  • Increase hiring efficiency
  • Tap into a network of global talents effectively

Yellow Elevator helps Candidates:

  • Job-hunt, particularly those who are busy and never have time to job-hunt by themselves
  • Gain access to greater job opportunities locally and globally

Yellow Elevator offers Recommenders:

  • The opportunity to get rewarded for every successful referral
  • The opportunity to help their network of friends find better jobs
