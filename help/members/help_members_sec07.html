7. Saved Jobs

  • This section enables you to view all your saved jobs.
  • 7.1. Search/Save Jobs & Refer Now
    • To search for jobs, go to the main page by clicking on the Yellow Elevator logo on the top left corner, or search at the search feature at the top of every page.
    • Fill in the search parameters and click ‘Search Jobs’.
    • The search results will be displayed if there are any job ads that match your search parameters.
    • Click on the jobs and you will be redirected to the job ad. You may choose to ‘Refer Now’ or ‘Save Job’.
    • If you click on ‘Refer Now’, a pop up window will appear with 3 questions. You are required to answer all 3 questions and when you are done, click on ‘Refer Now’.
    • If you click on ‘Save Job’ this job ad will be saved in your Saved Jobs, and you may refer this job ad to your contacts later at the Refer section.

  • 7.2. More about the Refer Now feature
    • The Refer Now button is located at the bottom right corner of every job ad published in the Yellow Elevator website.
    • This feature enables you to refer jobs to members right away (applies to non-members as well)
    • When using this feature to refer a job to a non-members, the non-member will automatically receive the job referral, as well as an invitation to become a member of Yellow Elevator at the same time.
