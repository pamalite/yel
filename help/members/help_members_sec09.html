9. Confirm Employment

  • This section allows you to see the date on which your resume is viewed by an employer. It also allows you to inform Yellow Elevator whenever you are employed by an employer by clicking on the 'Confirm Employed' button. You MUST click on the 'Confirm Employed' button whenever you are employed by an employer. Failure to do so may result in your contact not receiving their reward, or result in the termination of your membership with Yellow Elevator.
