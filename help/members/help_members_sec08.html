8. Refer

  • This section allows you to refer your Contacts to your Saved Jobs.
  • 8.1. Refer Saved Jobs to Your Contacts
    • Select a contact, then select a saved job and click ‘Refer’. A pop up window will appear with 3 questions. You are required to answer all 3 questions and when you are done, click on ‘Refer Now’.
    • If the desired contact is not in the list, you may enter the email address of the new contact at the fieldbox provided below the existing contacts.
    • The right column shows a brief summary of the saved job and your potential reward.
