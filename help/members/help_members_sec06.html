6. Contacts

  • This section enables you to add and edit your contacts. You can only find and add contacts who are already members of Yellow Elevator If you wish to add a non-member, invite them at Invite Contacts, or use the REFER NOW feature to refer the non-member right away.
  • 6.1. Adding an Existing Member into Your Contacts
    • Click on ‘Add Contact’.
    • Click ‘Find’ after you have entered the relevant information of your contact.
    • If your information matches any existing members in the system, a list will be displayed. Identify your desired contacts and click ‘Add’.
    • You will need to wait for your contacts to approve adding you before you can see them in your Contacts.

  • 6.2. Adding Non-Members into Your Contacts
    • You can only find and add contacts who are already members of Yellow Elevator If you wish to add a non-member, invite them at Invite Contacts, or use the REFER NOW feature to refer the non-member right away.

  • 6.3. Creating Networks
    • To create a network, first, go to the Networks tab and click on ‘Add Network’.
    • A window will pop up. Select an industry from the drop down menu. Once you have selected the industry, click on ‘Create Network’.
    • You have now created a network. To create more, follow the steps above.
    • To remove networks, select the networks by checking the checkboxes on the left, then click ‘Remove Selected Networks’.

  • 6.4. Add Your Contacts into Your Networks
    • To add your contacts into your networks, first select the contacts by checking the checkboxes situated at the left.
    • Then, click on the drop down menu and select the industry you wish to add your contacts to.
    • You may add a contact into more than 1 networks. To do so, repeat the steps above.

  • 6.5. View Contacts History
    • Contacts History contains all information regarding referrals that have been made to the individual contacts. This is a useful section to help you keep track of all your referrals.
    • To view your contacts history, just click on their names.
