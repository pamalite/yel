5. Resumes

  • This section allows you to create your online resume or choose to upload your existing one. You are able to upload multiple resumes for different job applications.
  • 5.1. Create New Resume
    • Click on ‘Create New Resume’.
    • A new window will appear. Fill the Cover Note Section. This section is for you to write a short summary of yourself for the Employers to view. When you are done, click on ‘Save & Next’.
    • You are now at the Work Experiences section. Click on ‘Add Work Experience’. Fill up the parameters accordingly. If you wish to add more work experience, simply repeat the process by clicking on ‘Add Work Experience’. Click on ‘Save & Next’ when you are done. Click on ‘Remove’ if you wish to remove any work experience.
    • You are now at the Education & Qualifications section. Click on ‘Add Education/Qualification’ and fill up the fields accordingly. If you wish to add more Education/Qualification, simply repeat the process by clicking on ‘Add Education/Qualification’. Click on ‘Save & Next’ when you are done. Click on ‘Remove’ if you wish to remove any Education/Qualification.
    • You are now at the General Skills section. Fill up the empty box with the skills that you possess. Click on ‘Save & Next’ when you are done.
    • You are now at the Technical Skills section. Click on ‘Add Technical Skills’ and fill up the fields accordingly. When you are done, click on ‘Save and Done’. By now, you have completed your online resume.

  • 5.2. Upload Resume
    • This section allows you to upload your resume directly in file formats. Click on ‘Browse’, choose the file that you wish to upload and click ‘Open’. After that, click on ‘Upload’ to upload your resume.
