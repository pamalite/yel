3. Job Ads

  • This section enables you to create job ads to publish on the Yellow Elevator website.
  • 3.1. Creating a New Job Ad
    • Click on ‘Add New Job Ad’.
    • Fill in the job title of the position that you wish to advertise.
    • Select an industry from the ‘Industry’ drop down menu.
    • Select the designated country for the new job ad from the ‘Country’ drop down menu.
    • Type in the Salary that you wish to pay for the New Job. Click on the Negotiable box below if it is negotiable.
    • Enter the job description in the box provided.
    • Click on ‘Publish’ once you are pleased with all the filled parameters. A pop up will appear for you to reconfirm your Job Ad. Click on ‘OK’ to publish or ‘Cancel’ to make more changes.

  • 3.2. The "Currently Open" Tab
    • This subsection displays jobs that are still not expired or still have vacancies, which is also the default page for Job Ads.

  • 3.3. The "Close Selected Jobs" button and the "Already Closed" Tab
    • The Already Closed subsection displays job ads that are (1) expired or, (2) when you close selected job ads and click on ‘Click Selected Jobs’.

  • 3.4. The "New From This Job" Button
    • Click on this button and you will arrive at the job ad you previously posted. This feature is designed for you to create new job ads from making slight changes to existing job ads so that you do not need to create a new job ad that is very similar to an existing job ad from scratch.
    • Click on ‘Publish’ once you are pleased with all the filled parameters. A pop up will appear for you to reconfirm your job ad. Click on ‘OK’ to publish or ‘Cancel’ to make more changes.
