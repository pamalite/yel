4. Referrals

  • This section gathers all the resumes of the referred candidates. You can view their resumes, and then employ the suitable candidates.
  • 4.1. Referrals
    • The number in the Referrals column shows the total number of resumes received for the particular job ad. Click on the number to view the resumes.

    • 4.1.1. Recommended
      • This sections enables you to view candidates recommended by Yellow Elevator’s matching system.
      • Click on ‘Testimonial’ to view a brief testimonial about the candidate.
      • Click on ‘Resume’ and a pop up screen will appear. You will be asked to confirm whether you agree to the terms governing your viewing of the downloaded resumes by Yellow Elevator. Click ‘OK’ if you have read, understood, and agreed to the terms and ‘Cancel’ if you haven’t.
      • If you click ‘OK’, a new window will pop up. You will have the option to ‘Save as XML’, ‘Save as PDF’, or ‘Print Resume’. Click ‘Close’ when you are done to close the pop up window.
      • Click on ‘Confirm Employed’ if you wish to employ the candidate. A window will pop up requesting for employment details of the candidate. Once you are done, click on ‘Confirm Employed’ to confirm the Employment and ‘Cancel’ to cancel.
      • Click on ‘Shortlist’ to shortlist candidates. The Shortlist feature enables you to arrange and narrow down to your preferred candidates. The candidate will then be moved to the Shortlist tab.

    • 4.1.2. Other
      • This section lists the resumes that did not have successful matchings with the job ads, as well as, lists all the file format resumes.

    • 4.1.3. My Shortlist
      • This section enables you to arrange and narrow down your preferred candidates. Similar to the Recommended tab, click on ‘Confirm Employed’ if you wish to employ a shortlisted candidate. A window will pop up requesting for employment details of the candidate. Click on ‘Confirm Employed’ to confirm the Employment and ‘Cancel’ to cancel.
