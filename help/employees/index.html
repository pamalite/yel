Help Section - Employees System


1 .My Profile
  • This section enables you to edit/update your profile.
 
 
2. My Employer
  • This section enables you to manage the employers that you are responsible to serve. Here you may create accounts for new employers, set employer account details e.g. service fees, extra charges (if any); as well as deactivate the employers' accounts if required.
 
  • To set up an account for a new employer, the first step is to click on 'Add New Employer', fill in the employer’s profile, then click on 'Save and Update Profile'. Then, you MUST set up the Service Fee. To do so, click on the Service Fee tab, followed by 'Add New Fee' to fill in the service fees according to the Agreement/Contract with the employer. If there is any extra charges attached, click on the Extra Charges tab followed by ‘Add New Charge’ and fill in the figures accordingly.
 
  • To update existing employer accounts, click on 'Update' and change the relevant fields. Once you are done, click on ‘Save and Update Profile.’
 
  • To create a new account based on information from an existing Employer, click on 'New From This Employer'.  Continue with the steps similar to the ones you would take in setting up an account for a new employer. 
 
  • To deactivate an employer account, check the box on the left of the User ID, and click on 'Deactivate Selected Employers'.
 
 
3. Photos
  • This section enables you to approve photos submitted by Members. Members' photos will not be displayed in their accounts without your approval.
 
 
4. Invoices & Receipts
  • This section enables you to keep track of all the invoices we have sent to the employers, and follow up with them on the payments. The red symbol on the left indicates that the invoice due date is up. Always follow up closely with employers who are late with their payments.
      
  • To view invoices, click on the invoice number on the Invoice column. A pop up window will appear displaying the invoice. You may choose to print the invoice or save it as a PDF file.
     
  • If an employer has made payment, click on 'Confirm Payment'. A pop up window will appear. Fill in the relevant payment information. Then click on the 'Confirm Payment' button in the pop up window once you are done.
     
  • You may view the invoice in PDF format. To do so, click on the PDF file logo on the right.
 
 
5. Rewards
  • This section enables you to keep track of all the rewards that we have to pay to the members who have made successful referrals.
 
  • When rewards have been paid to the Members, you need to record the details of payment into this section. To do so, click on 'Confirm'. A pop up window will appear. Fill in the payment details and then click on 'Confirm Payment'.
      
  • The Partially Paid tab keeps track of all partially paid rewards.
      
  • The Fully Paid tab keeps track of all fully paid rewards. (Note: We normally pay the rewards in full, very seldom partially.)

  • Notice: Rewards information will only be displayed in this section based on 2 conditions:
    • Payment from employer has been received and processed.
    • The guarantee period of the hired candidate is over.
 
6. Bonus
  • This section enables you to keep track of all the bonuses that we have to pay to the members (candidates) who are successfully employed by the employers.
  • Notice: Bonus information will only be displayed in this section based on 1 condition:
    • Rewards have been paid to the members (referrers).

7. Referrals
  • This section contains 2 significant features.
      
  • The first is the Offer Letter (OL) feature. This feature enables you keep track of whether you have received the signed offer letter from the employer whenever there is a successful employment of a candidate. When you receive the signed offer letter, whether by fax, email, or any other means, please remember to click on 'OL'.
     
  • The second is a feature for you to track whether employers have submitted employment information in 'Employ Now' (a section located in the Employers system). Without submission of employment information in 'Employ Now', our system will not send an invoice to the employer. As such, your duty is to make sure the employers take the above mentioned action whenever they have successfully employed a candidate.
     
  • This is how it works: when you see an entry (date) under the 'Member Confirmed Employment' column but do no see any entry (date) under the 'Employed On' column, that means the candidate claims that he/she has been employed by the employer; however, the employer has yet to inform us about it by making a submission in ‘Employ Now’. As such, you need to first contact the candidate to verify if he/she has indeed been employed to a position by the employer. If the candidate verifies his/her employment, get the appropriate employment details and contact the employer for verification. If a successful employment has been made, request for the employer to submit the employment details in ‘Employ Now’.
     
  • To track whether the Employer has made the submission, just check the 'Employed On' column to see if there are any new entries.
     
  • The contact information of the candidates and the employers can be found by just clicking on their names.
 
 
8. Replacement
  • We offer a free replacement to employers if the initial candidate that they employed is terminated due to any appropriate reasons. This section enables you to authorize replacements for employers. However, before you authorize a replacement, you need to get the employer to fax, email, or submit hard copies of documentary proofs that the initial candidate has been terminated. Otherwise, you MUST NOT authorize a replacement.
  • To authorize a replacement, click on 'Authorize Replacement' to perform the action.
 
 
9. Employers
  • This section enables you to reset passwords for the Employers if requested by them. Click on 'Reset Password' to perform this action.
 
 
10. Members
  • This section enables you to reset passwords of the Members if requested by them. Click on 'Reset Password' to perform this action.
